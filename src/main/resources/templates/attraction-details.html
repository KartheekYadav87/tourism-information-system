Travel Tips

  • Arrive early in the morning to avoid crowds and experience the site in peace.
  • Wear comfortable footwear as you may need to walk considerable distances.
  • Dress modestly, especially for religious and heritage sites — cover shoulders and knees.
  • Carry sufficient water and sunscreen, especially during April–June.
  • Hire a local government-certified guide for a richer historical and cultural experience.
  • Check official timings before visiting as they may change during festivals and holidays.
