About Tamil Nadu Tourism

Preserving heritage, protecting nature, and promoting sustainable travel experiences across the Enchanting State of Tamil Nadu.

Our Mission

Showcasing Tamil Nadu to the World

The Tamil Nadu Tourism Development Corporation (TTDC) was incorporated in 1971 with the primary objective of promoting tourism in Tamil Nadu by building tourism infrastructure on commercial basis. Today, we manage a vast network of hotels, conduct package tours, and maintain pristine tourist spots.

Our mission is to provide world-class facilities for tourists visiting the state while preserving the rich cultural heritage, ecological balance, and traditional values of Tamil Nadu. We strive to make every traveler's experience magical, safe, and sustainable.

  • Heritage Preservation: Safeguarding our invaluable archeological sites.
  • Eco-Tourism: Promoting travel that respects and nurtures nature.
  • Cultural Exchange: Connecting global travelers with local traditions.

Our Impact

Why Travel With Us?

Community First

Our initiatives directly support local artisans, guides, and small businesses. When you travel through TN Tourism, you contribute to the livelihood of thousands of families across the state.

Safety & Trust

As the official state tourism board, your safety is our top priority. We vet all our partner hotels and guides, run 24x7 tourist helplines, and maintain a dedicated Tourist Police force.

Sustainable Future

We are committed to reducing the carbon footprint of tourism. From plastic-free zones in hill stations to protecting marine reserves in the Gulf of Mannar, we champion green travel.
